Episode III – Revenge of the Sith

"Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith" is a 2005 epic space opera film directed by George Lucas and is the sixth film released in the Star Wars franchise. The film stars Ewan McGregor as Obi-Wan Kenobi, Hayden Christensen as Anakin Skywalker/Darth Vader, and Natalie Portman as Padmé Amidala.

The plot of the film is set during the Clone Wars, a conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Separatist Alliance. The film follows the story of Anakin Skywalker's gradual fall to the dark side of the Force and his transformation into Darth Vader, one of the most iconic villains in cinematic history. The film also depicts the events leading up to the birth of Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia Organa.

"Revenge of the Sith" received generally positive reviews from critics and audiences, who praised the film's stunning visuals, action sequences, and emotional depth. The film was also a commercial success, grossing over $850 million worldwide. The film's impact on popular culture cannot be understated, as it concluded the prequel trilogy and marked the end of George Lucas' involvement with the franchise until the Disney era. The film is considered by many fans to be one of the best Star Wars films, and is a must-watch for fans of the franchise.

Screen time: 2h 20m
