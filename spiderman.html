Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021)

"Spider-Man: No Way Home" is an upcoming superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. It is the sequel to "Spider-Man: Homecoming" (2017) and "Spider-Man: Far From Home" (2019), and is directed by Jon Watts. The film is set to star Tom Holland as Spider-Man, along with a star-studded cast that includes Zendaya, Jacob Batalon, Marisa Tomei, Benedict Cumberbatch, and others.

The plot of the film has not been officially revealed, but it has been heavily speculated that the film will involve the concept of the multiverse, with previous actors who have played Spider-Man, such as Tobey Maguire and Andrew Garfield, making appearances. There have also been rumors of appearances from other characters from previous SpiSder-Man films, such as Doctor Octopus and Electro.

"Spider-Man: No Way Home" is scheduled to be released on December 17, 2021, and has already generated a lot of excitement and anticipation among fans of the Marvel Cinematic Universe and the Spider-Man franchise.

Screen time: 2h 28m

Cast: Tom Holland, Andrew Garfield, Tobey Maguire, Zendaya, Benedict Cumberbatch, Jacob Batalon, Jon Favreau, Jamie Foxx, Willem Dafoe, Alfred Molina, Benedict Wong, Tony Revolori, Marisa Tomei
