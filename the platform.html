The Platform

"The Platform" (original title: "El Hoyo") is a Spanish science fiction horror film that was released on Netflix in 2020. Directed by Galder Gaztelu-Urrutia and starring Ivan Massagué, Zorion Eguileor, and Antonia San Juan, the film takes place in a dystopian future where prisoners are housed in a vertical tower with one cell per level.

The premise of the film revolves around a platform that descends from the top of the tower to the bottom, carrying food for the prisoners. However, as it descends, the food becomes scarcer, and those at the bottom levels are left with little to eat, while those at the top indulge in excess. The film explores themes of inequality, social class, and the human condition, as the characters struggle to survive and maintain their morality in a brutal and unforgiving systemS.

"The Platform" received critical acclaim for its innovative storytelling, thought-provoking themes, and strong performances. It has been praised for its ability to create tension and suspense, as well as its use of gore and violence to comment on contemporary social issues. Overall, it is a must-watch for fans of science fiction, horror, and dystopian narratives.

Screen time: 1h 34m

Cast: Ivan Massagué, Zorion Eguileor, Antonia San Juan, Emilio Buale, Alexandra Masangkay, Zihara Llana, Mario Pardo
