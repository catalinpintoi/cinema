Her (2013)

"Her" is a 2013 American science-fiction romantic drama film directed by Spike Jonze, who also wrote the screenplay. The film stars Joaquin Phoenix as Theodore Twombly, a lonely and introverted man who falls in love with an artificially intelligent operating system named Samantha, voiced by Scarlett Johansson.

Set in a near-future Los Angeles, the film explores themes of love, relationships, and the nature of consciousness in the age of technology. Through Theodore's interactions with Samantha, the film delves into the complexities of human emotions and the challenges of human-machine relationships. The film's cinematography and production design are also noteworthy, creating a visually stunning and immersive world that adds to the film's emotional depth.

"Her" was critically acclaimed upon its release, and received numerous awards and nominations, including five Academy Award nominations and a win for Best Original Screenplay. The film has been praised for its originality, thought-provoking themes, and powerful performances, particularly by Phoenix and Johansson. It is a must-watch for fans of science fiction, romance, and thought-provoking dramas.

Screen time: 2h 6m

Cast: Joaquin Phoenix, Lynn Adrianna Freedman, Lisa Renee Pitts, Gabe Gomez, Chris Pratt, Artt Butler, May Lindstrom, Rooney Mara, Bill Hader
